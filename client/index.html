What is LoopQuest?

LoopQuest is a multiplayer game inviting you to explore a world of adventure from your Web browser, while utilizing your Loopring NFTs.

LoopQuest is a fork of BrowserQuest, an open Source MMO delevoped by Mozilla.

Learn more about the technology

LoopQuest is available on Firefox, Chrome, Safari as well as iOS devices and Firefox for Android.
